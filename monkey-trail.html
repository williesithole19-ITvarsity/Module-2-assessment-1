MONKEY-TRAIL

A beaten path where children play.

Monkey Trail is a 16.1 kilometer moderately trafficked loop trail located near Keithville, Louisiana that features beautiful wild flowers and is rated as moderate. The trail is primarily used for hiking, running, horses, and mountain biking and is accessible year-round. Dogs are also able to use this trail but must be kept on leash.

Where are they found

They are found most often in savannas, grasslands, and forests, but they occupy a wide range of habitats, including deserts, swamps, and highlands in tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and Asia.
