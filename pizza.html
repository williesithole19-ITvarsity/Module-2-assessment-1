A restaurant or takeout where pizzas are made and sold as main food is called a pizzeria (Italian pronunciation: [ˌpittseˈriːa]) or simply “pizza parlor” in English.

The term pizza pie is a (mainly American) dialectal, and pie is used for simplicity in some contexts, such as among pizzeria staff.
